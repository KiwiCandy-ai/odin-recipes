Shakshuka Recipe

Shakshuka is a North African and Middle Eastern dish featuring poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce with onions, bell peppers, and garlic, often spiced with cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper, and commonly served with bread.

Ingredients

  • Onions, dices
  • Peppers, diced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomatoes, tinned
  • Eggs
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Chilli Powder
  • Coriander, chopped

Instructions

  1. Add peppers, onions and garlic to a saut√© pan. Cooke until onion is translucent.
  2. Add spices and cook for one minute.
  3. Pour in can of tomatoes and bring to a simmer.
  4. Use a large spoon to make small wells in the sauce and crack the eggs into each well.
  5. Cook the eggs for 5 to 8 minutes.
  6. Garnish with corinader before serving.
