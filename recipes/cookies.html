Cookies

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal or raisins as well.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar together, then beat in the egg.
  2. Mix in the flour and chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop 10 large tbsps of the mixture onto a baking tray.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes, then leave to cool.
