Lasagna

A classic Italian baked pasta dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternating with fillings of ragu, b√©chamel, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Marinara
  • Onions, chopped
  • Bechamel
  • Cheddar, grated

Instructions

  1. In a saute pan cook beef mince and onions in a tbsp of oil.
  2. When mince is brown and onions are soft add marinara sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Start layering a rectangle baking dish with the lasagna sheets, then add a layer of the beef, a layer of bechamel and top with cheese.
  4. Keep layering like this until all the beef is used up and finish the final with a topping of cheese.
  5. Cover dish with foil and bake in the oven until bubbling.
